Lasagna Recipe

Description

Step 1: Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch). Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce. Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.

Step 2: Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar Prego Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
